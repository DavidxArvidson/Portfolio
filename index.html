About

I got my first taste for coding in middle school computer class working with some very basic HTML. I continued to play around with the concept of coding over the years on and off while I pursued other avenues. After graduating from college and exploring various career options, software development popped up as a recommendation from trusted friends who knew how my brain worked. I thought back to middle school computer class and decided to give it a try and I haven't looked back since!
